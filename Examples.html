Over 25,000 customers needed to be vetted to determine if they were active, if their details were correct, if there were duplicates of the account, and if they were purchasing via our online platform. I worked with account reps and management to detemermine the best course of action, and held weekly updates with the data team to ensure that changes were completed in a timely manner.

Further, at Dematic, I had a similar task. I often worked with co-workers to determine the right course of action to clean up the customer data, as well as create a process to prevent this cleanup from being needed in the future.

Testing and Requirement Analysis

I was the main tester for Pratt & Whitney Component Solutions for the online ordering platform. Because of the various updates, I was testing quite frequently and have become very familiar with determining test case scenarios and potential solutions, performing tests, and reporting outputs to developers.
